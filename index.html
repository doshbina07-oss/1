Canada’s Premier Hotels and Resorts in 2026

Across Canada, premier hotels and resorts combine upscale accommodation with engaging leisure experiences, drawing visitors who want relaxation and recreation in a single destination. These properties typically showcase beautifully curated communal spaces, elegant architecture, and a strong connection to their local environment. Designed with professionalism and guest comfort in mind, they create an atmosphere that feels serene yet refined, supported by attentive hospitality and highly organized operations.

Many of these resorts are developed to balance leisure and convenience. Spacious rooms and thoughtfully designed suites are complemented by easy access to on-site boutique shopping, art collections, and curated experiences. Dining experiences range from informal, locally inspired cafés to elegant, award-winning restaurants, while wellness facilities—including world-class spas, thermal pools, and fitness centers—offer opportunities to unwind. Guided outdoor excursions, seasonal events, and cultural programming further enrich the overall stay, ensuring each visit feels memorable and varied.

Fairmont Niagara Falls (The Grand Château) – Niagara Falls, Ontario

Experience & Culture

This iconic property features a rich tradition of grand Canadian hospitality, offering guests immersive historical tours and curated access to the region's famous viticulture. Guests can enjoy a classic, refined environment that celebrates the grandeur of the local landscape.

Accommodation & Amenities

The resort provides stylish, modern guest rooms and expansive luxury suites, many offering panoramic, floor-to-ceiling views of Niagara Falls. Wellness amenities include a full-service therapeutic spa, an advanced fitness center, and a heated indoor infinity pool overlooking the gorge.

Dining

A diverse collection of restaurants caters to a wide range of preferences, from upscale farm-to-table dining experiences utilizing Niagara-region produce to relaxed, casual bistros and artisan wine bars.

Location

Situated steps from the edge of Niagara Falls, the property perfectly blends a prestigious, luxury retreat with scenic, natural surroundings.

The Westin Bayshore – Vancouver / Richmond Region, British Columbia

Experience & Culture

This premium waterfront resort delivers an experience centered entirely on holistic wellness and active lifestyle recreation. The property boasts extensive indoor and outdoor wellness spaces designed to connect guests with the refreshing Pacific coast air.

Accommodation & Amenities

Rooms and suites feature contemporary, biophilic décor focused on comfort, natural light, and practicality. Guests have seamless access to a signature spa, heated outdoor and indoor pools, specialized fitness facilities, and direct connections to local urban centers and downtown Vancouver.

Dining

On-site restaurants offer a vibrant mix of international dishes and Pacific Northwest regional cuisine, ranging from ocean-fresh fine dining to casual, health-conscious fare.

Location

Overlooking the tranquil waterfront, the resort beautifully combines coastal mountain scenery with a relaxed yet energetic West Coast setting.

Hotel Bonaventure – Montréal, Quebec

Experience & Culture

As a striking example of unique urban architecture, this property provides a peaceful sanctuary right in the heart of the city, complete with acres of stunning rooftop gardens, wandering streams, and resident wildlife.

Accommodation & Amenities

Visitors enjoy refined, executive-style accommodations seamlessly integrated with downtown Montréal, making it effortless to combine a relaxing resort environment with vibrant city exploration, shopping, and historical tours.

Dining

Several refined restaurants, bistros, and chic lounges are located within the venue, offering culinary creations heavily inspired by Québec’s distinctive, French-infused culinary heritage.

Location

Positioned atop a major downtown complex, the hotel enjoys a unique rooftop vantage point that reflects Montréal’s dynamic cultural character and metropolitan energy.

JW Marriott The Rosseau Muskoka Resort & Spa – Minett / Orillia Region, Ontario

Experience & Culture

This luxury resort offers an authentic, high-end wilderness retreat, pairing sophisticated interior design with modern outdoor adventure programming like snowshoeing, boating, and stargazing.

Accommodation & Amenities

Comfortable, oversized rooms and multi-room suites are paired with premium amenities such as a luxury full-service spa, indoor/outdoor swimming pools, and a dedicated fitness studio. The surrounding natural landscape enhances the resort’s peaceful, restorative atmosphere.

Dining

Guests can select from multiple distinct dining venues, ranging from sophisticated, fine-dining steakhouses to laid-back, lakeside eateries that highlight fresh, regional flavors.

Location

Located in the heart of Ontario’s scenic lake country, the resort provides a welcoming, prestigious destination where luxury entertainment meets untouched natural beauty.

Key Characteristics of Canadian Hotels and Resorts

Premium hotels and resorts throughout Canada often combine contemporary luxury design with enduring architectural elements that honor their natural surroundings. Many properties also feature state-of-the-art conference facilities and elegant event spaces, making them exceptionally suitable for business gatherings, special occasions, and leisure travel alike. Some venues provide exclusive club-level floors and personalized concierge services for those seeking an elevated, highly tailored experience.

By integrating lodging, fine dining, cultural entertainment, and holistic wellness amenities in one seamless location, these resorts offer a convenient and comprehensive getaway. With professionally operated facilities and close proximity to major geographic attractions, these properties continue to contribute significantly to Canada’s international tourism industry.

Whether planning a short weekend retreat or an extended seasonal vacation, visitors can anticipate reliable service, exceptional accommodation, and diverse recreational options all within a single destination.
